Write an article about: $ARGUMENTS

## Process

1. Research the topic for depth and accuracy
2. Create an outline with clear narrative arc
3. Write the full article with engaging intro and strong conclusion
4. Apply SEO best practices
5. Self-review for clarity and flow

## Output

- Suggested title (compelling, SEO-friendly)
- Meta description (under 160 characters)
- Full article in markdown
- Suggested tags/categories
