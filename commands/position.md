Define or refine positioning for: $ARGUMENTS

## Process

1. Read existing strategy documents (personas, competitive analysis, positioning) in `strategy/` and any available product context in `CLAUDE.md`
2. Research the competitive landscape using web search
3. Work through the six-component positioning framework in order:
   - Competitive alternatives
   - Unique attributes
   - Value mapping
   - Target customer characteristics
   - Market category
   - Relevant trends
4. Complete the positioning canvas
5. Derive a positioning statement from the canvas
6. Document messaging implications

## Output

### Positioning Canvas
Completed canvas with all six components filled in, including evidence.

### Positioning Statement
A concise statement synthesized from the canvas components.

### Messaging Implications
Primary message, supporting messages, and proof points derived from the positioning.

### Next Steps
How to apply this positioning to marketing copy, sales materials, and product decisions.
