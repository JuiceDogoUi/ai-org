# SOX Compliance

## Relevance to Software
SOX (Sarbanes-Oxley) applies to IT systems that affect financial reporting in publicly traded companies.

## Key Controls
- **Access Control**: Role-based access, principle of least privilege
- **Change Management**: All changes tracked, reviewed, approved
- **Audit Trail**: Complete logging of who did what and when
- **Segregation of Duties**: No single person controls entire process
- **Data Integrity**: Validation, checksums, reconciliation

## Technical Requirements
- [ ] Authentication and authorization on all financial systems
- [ ] Audit logging for all data modifications
- [ ] Change management process with approvals
- [ ] Regular access reviews
- [ ] Backup and disaster recovery tested
- [ ] Encryption for financial data at rest and in transit
