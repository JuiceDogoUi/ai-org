# Email Copy Templates

## Welcome Email
- Subject: Welcome to [Product] -- here's how to get started
- Body: Thank them, single next step, link to getting started guide
- CTA: "Set up your first [thing]"

## Feature Announcement
- Subject: [New] [Feature name] -- [benefit in 5 words]
- Body: What's new, why it matters to them, how to use it
- CTA: "Try [feature name]"

## Re-engagement
- Subject: We miss you -- here's what's new
- Body: 2-3 improvements since they left, easy path back
- CTA: "See what's changed"

## Rules
- Subject lines: under 50 characters, no ALL CAPS, no spam words
- Preview text: extends the subject, doesn't repeat it
- One primary CTA per email
- Plain text alternative for every HTML email
- Unsubscribe link visible and functional
