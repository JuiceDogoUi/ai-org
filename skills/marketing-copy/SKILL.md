# Marketing Copy

## Principles
- Lead with the benefit, not the feature
- Address the reader directly ("you" not "users")
- Create urgency without being manipulative
- One CTA per section -- don't split attention
- Social proof increases trust (testimonials, numbers, logos)

## Voice Guidelines
- Confident but not arrogant
- Clear but not dumbed down
- Friendly but not unprofessional
- Specific but not overwhelming

## Headlines
- Promise a clear benefit
- Be specific: "Reduce deploy time by 80%" > "Deploy faster"
- Use numbers when possible
- Keep under 10 words
