# Landing Page Copy Structure

## Hero Section
- **Headline**: Clear value proposition (10 words max)
- **Subheadline**: How you deliver that value (20 words max)
- **CTA**: Single, specific action ("Start free trial", "Get started free")
- **Social proof**: "Trusted by 10,000+ teams" or logos

## Problem Section
- Describe the pain point the reader experiences
- Use the reader's own language
- 2-3 bullet points maximum

## Solution Section
- Show how your product solves the problem
- Feature -> Benefit format: "Automated testing -> Ship with confidence"
- Include screenshot or demo

## Social Proof Section
- Customer testimonials with name, role, company
- Specific results: "Reduced our deploy time from 2 hours to 15 minutes"
- Logos of recognizable customers

## CTA Section
- Repeat the primary CTA
- Remove risk: "Free for 14 days, no credit card required"
- Address the main objection
