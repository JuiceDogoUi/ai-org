# SEO Guidelines

## On-Page SEO
- Title tag: 50-60 characters, keyword near the front
- Meta description: 120-160 characters, compelling, includes keyword
- URL slug: short, descriptive, hyphenated (`/how-to-optimize-queries`)
- H1: One per page, matches search intent
- H2-H3: Include secondary keywords naturally

## Content Optimization
- Target one primary keyword per article
- Include keyword in first 100 words
- Use related terms and synonyms naturally
- Aim for comprehensive coverage of the topic
- Update content regularly to keep it fresh

## Technical SEO
- Canonical URLs to prevent duplicate content
- Structured data (JSON-LD) for rich snippets
- Image optimization (WebP, descriptive filenames, alt text)
- Fast page load (under 3 seconds)
