# Article Templates

## How-To Article
1. Introduction: What you'll learn and why it matters
2. Prerequisites: What the reader needs before starting
3. Step-by-step instructions with code/screenshots
4. Common mistakes and troubleshooting
5. Conclusion: Summary and next steps

## Comparison Article
1. Introduction: What you're comparing and why
2. Criteria for comparison
3. Option A: Overview, pros, cons
4. Option B: Overview, pros, cons
5. Side-by-side summary table
6. Recommendation by use case

## Thought Leadership
1. Opening insight or contrarian take
2. Supporting evidence (data, case studies, experience)
3. Counterarguments and nuance
4. Practical implications
5. Call to action or forward-looking conclusion
