# Competitive Analysis

## Framework
1. **Identify competitors**: Direct, indirect, and potential
2. **Gather data**: Features, pricing, positioning, reviews, market share
3. **Analyze**: Strengths, weaknesses, gaps, opportunities
4. **Synthesize**: Strategic recommendations

## Key Dimensions
- Feature comparison (what they offer vs. what we offer)
- Pricing and business model
- Target audience and positioning
- Technology stack and architecture
- User experience and design quality
- Market presence and momentum

## Output
- Comparison matrix (features x competitors)
- SWOT per competitor
- Gap analysis (where they're weak, where we can differentiate)
- Strategic recommendations
